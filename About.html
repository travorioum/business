Welcome to Travorium Ltd!

Onafriq is Africa’s largest digital payments network, connecting mobile money users, banks, and financial services across more than 40 African countries.

The company is headquartered in London, UK, with a regional office in Port-Louis, Mauritius, supporting operations and regulatory compliance.

Founded by Daré Okoudjou, Onafriq aims to make African borders matter less by enabling fast, easy, and secure cross-border payments. With over 10 years of experience, Onafriq has built a strong reputation across the continent.
